FAQ

What is a Zip Bomb?

A Zip Bomb is a compressed file designed to use up a significant amount of system resources when decompressed. When unsuspecting users open the file, it expands to an enormous size, potentially causing their system to slow down or even crash.

Is Nuke.zip legal?

We're not lawyers. Don't be evil.

Yes, with our Dynamite and Nuclear plans, you can customize the detonation timer of your Zip Bomb. Additionally, Nuclear plan subscribers have access to exclusive Zip Bomb designs and the ability to beta test new features.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, log in to your account and navigate to the "Billing" section. From there, you can manage your subscription and cancel at any time. If you need assistance, please contact our support team.

What's the purpose of this?

The purpose of this site is to protest the use of .zip as a top-level domain. It's risky and can be used to introduce malicious functionality into scripting where .zip file operations may be involved.
